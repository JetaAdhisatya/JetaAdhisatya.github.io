The Story

ABABA Steak has been serving the best and only the best to customers since 2002. We have always been making customers happy with a full belly since our first and only site in Denpasar. Our unique methods of grilling will ensure the steaks are tasty as it will ever get. You will have a phenomenal experience when you are eating in ABABA Steak, because we always get our high quality meats imported from Japan, Australia, and United States. ABABA Steak serves steak menu in choice of beef, chicken, lamb, and fish. We also present a wide selection of side dishes and drinks that would fit to eat with the steak. Although we are currently open only in Denpasar, we are looking forward to expand our business and open in other major cities in Indonesia.
